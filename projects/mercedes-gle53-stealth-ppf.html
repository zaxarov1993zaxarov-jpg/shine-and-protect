The Story

Project Description

Elegance deserves lasting protection.

This Mercedes-AMG GLE 53 Coupe received a full-body Stealth Paint Protection Film installation, transforming its factory finish into a refined satin appearance while protecting every painted surface and select gloss black trim from rock chips, road debris, scratches, and everyday wear.

To further enhance the vehicle's appearance, the painted section of the roof was wrapped in gloss black Paint Protection Film, creating a cleaner and more aggressive contrast.

Stealth PPF delivers the perfect balance of aesthetics and performance by preserving the original paint beneath a self-healing protective layer while giving the vehicle a sophisticated satin finish.

At Shine & Protect LLC, every installation is completed with precision craftsmanship and uncompromising attention to detail.
