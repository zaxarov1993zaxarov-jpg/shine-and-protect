Our Services in Detail

Professional installation processes and comprehensive benefits explained

Paint Protection Film Installation

Paint Protection Film installation is a specialized process requiring precision and expertise. At Shine & Protect, we ensure every inch of your vehicle receives comprehensive defense against road hazards, rock chips, and environmental damage.

Our advanced PPF solutions offer self-healing properties, crystal-clear transparency, and unmatched durability that keeps your vehicle looking showroom-fresh for years.

Our Installation Process

1
Consultation & Planning

We evaluate your vehicle's vulnerable areas and customize protection strategy to match your needs and budget.

2
Surface Preparation

Thorough washing, clay bar treatment, and alcohol wipe-down ensure flawless adhesion.

3
Precision Application

Computer-cut patterns ensure seamless coverage across complex curves and body lines.

4
Heat Activation

Controlled heat activates adhesive and molds film around intricate contours.

5
Quality Inspection

Thorough inspection under professional lighting ensures perfection before delivery.

Professional Ceramic Coating

Ceramic coating represents the pinnacle of automotive surface protection. This advanced nanotechnology creates a permanent bond with your vehicle's paint, forming an impenetrable barrier against contaminants, UV damage, and chemical etching.

Professional-grade ceramic coatings provide 5+ years of protection with proper maintenance, delivering a showroom shine that resists fading and water spots.

Full Body Stealth Paint Protection Film

Elegance deserves lasting protection.

This Mercedes-AMG GLE 53 Coupe received a full-body Stealth Paint Protection Film installation, transforming its factory finish into a refined satin appearance while protecting every painted surface and select gloss black trim from rock chips, road debris, scratches, and everyday wear.
