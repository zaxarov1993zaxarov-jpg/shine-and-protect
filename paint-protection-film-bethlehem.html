Frequently Asked Questions

How long does Paint Protection Film last?

Most premium films last between 10 and 12 years when properly maintained.

Can PPF be removed?

Yes. Professionally installed film can be removed without damaging factory paint.

Does PPF replace ceramic coating?

No. PPF protects against physical damage while ceramic coating mainly improves cleaning and gloss. Many owners combine both.

Will the film turn yellow?

Modern premium films are designed to resist yellowing and include manufacturer warranties.
